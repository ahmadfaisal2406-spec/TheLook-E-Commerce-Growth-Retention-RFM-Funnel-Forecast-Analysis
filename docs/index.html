Project Overview

An end-to-end analytics project simulating a large-scale e-commerce platform. Using the BigQuery public dataset thelook_ecommerce, this project covers the full data analyst workflow:

  • Data extraction and aggregation with SQL (BigQuery)
  • Cohort retention analysis
  • Clickstream conversion funnel analysis
  • Time-series forecasting with Python (Facebook Prophet)
  • Executive interactive dashboard in Looker Studio

Milestones & Key Findings

Business Growth

GMV grew over 120,000% from Jan 2019 to May 2026 ($382 to $462K/month), driven purely by buyer volume rather than AOV increases.

Cohort Retention

Revealed an onboarding crisis with only 1-2% first-month retention, though retained users proved highly loyal up to month 10.

Funnel Analysis

Identified a major 57.96% drop-off from Cart to Purchase. YouTube emerged as the most efficient traffic source for conversions.

GMV Forecasting

Projected an average GMV of $350k/month for the next 6 months, identifying clear peak (April-May) and low seasonality trends.

Strategic Recommendations

  • Loyalty Program: Build an onboarding and email sequence to lower the 98% initial churn rate.
  • Budget Optimization: Shift ad spend from low-season (Jun-Jul, Nov) to peak season (Dec-Jan).
  • AOV Improvement: Implement product bundling and free-shipping minimums to boost the stagnant $83 AOV.
  • Channel Reallocation: Increase YouTube Ads budget as it delivers the best conversion intent.
